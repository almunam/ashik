Read Beyond the Stars

A social media posts series for Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn promoting ethical review practices for consumers and hospitality businesses.

Campaign Strategy

Read Beyond the Stars translates the capstone research into short, accessible social media posts for two audiences: consumers deciding whether to trust online reviews, and hospitality professionals managing digital reputation. The series is designed with flexible post formats suitable for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and other visual-first social platforms, creating a cohesive campaign that can reach both everyday travellers and industry professionals.

Campaign Architecture

Primary Audience

Consumers: Millennial and Gen Z travellers (ages 22-42) who rely on online reviews when booking accommodation. Active on Instagram and Facebook.

Professionals: Hotel managers, tourism marketers, and digital communication practitioners seeking ethical reputation management guidance. Active on LinkedIn.

Campaign Purpose

Translate capstone research findings into publicly accessible, platform-native content that (1) builds review literacy among everyday travellers, (2) discourages fake review inflation and manipulative reputation practices, and (3) demonstrates the academic value of the capstone research to a broader audience.

Platforms

Social media posts for public education and professional awareness across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and other visual-first platforms.

Core Message

Ratings are not neutral. Platform structure, fake reviews, and management responses shape trust.

Estimated KPIs & Performance Targets

Benchmark Sources

Targets are based on 2025 industry benchmarks: Instagram median engagement 1.0-3.0% for educational content (RivalIQ / Sprout Social 2025); LinkedIn education sector engagement 3.0-3.6% (Closely 2025). A new account without paid promotion is expected to perform at or slightly above median.

Reach per Post

500-1,000
impressions per post across all platforms combined. Organic baseline for a new educational account with no prior following.

Engagement Rate

1.0-3.0% Instagram / Facebook
3.0-3.6% LinkedIn
Likes + comments + shares divided by reach. Educational image posts benchmark: ~1.05% (top 25%) on IG.

Saves & Shares

5-15 saves or shares per post on Instagram. Saves signal high-value educational content and improve algorithmic reach over time.

Follower Growth

+50-100 net new followers across all platforms by campaign close (Day 30). Benchmark: 3.3% monthly growth rate for small accounts.

Profile Visits

200+ total profile visits across Instagram and LinkedIn over the 19-day campaign. Indicates interest beyond a single post.

Link Clicks / Bio Visits

2-5% click-through rate on posts that include a call-to-action directing audiences to a bio link or capstone resource. Baseline for educational CTAs.
